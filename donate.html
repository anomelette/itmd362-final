DONATIONS – SECOND STORY BOOKS

Your donations mean a lot to us

Every book you donate helps keep our shelves full, our events thriving, and our community connected. Your generosity gives each story a second life—whether it becomes someone’s new favorite read, sparks a conversation at an open mic, or inspires creativity in our shared space.

How to donate

Donating is simple! Just bring your gently used books to our store during open hours and a staff member will help you with drop-off. We welcome fiction, nonfiction, poetry, children’s books, and more.

Not sure if your items are a good fit? Give us a call or send a quick message — we’re always happy to help!

Thank you for helping our community grow, one book at a time.
